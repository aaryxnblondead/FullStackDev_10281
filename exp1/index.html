About Me

I'm Aaryan Singh, a Computer Science Engineering student at Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering, with a strong foundation in data analysis, front-end development, and programming. My passion for debating, public speaking, and philosophy complements my leadership in competitions focused on legal research and simulated hearings.

An avid learner with interests in music production, illustration, and sports (having represented India in handball), I thrive at the intersection of technology, creativity, and community engagement.
