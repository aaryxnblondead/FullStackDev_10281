About Me

I’m Aaryan Singh, a Computer Science Engineering student at Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering, with a strong foundation in data analysis, front-end development, and programming. My technical expertise includes web development for the Student Council website, focusing on creating dynamic, user-friendly interfaces. Beyond academics, I’ve taken on dynamic roles as a Technical Representative, Webmaster, and Outreach Manager, contributing to events like Crescendo, our flagship technical festival, and community initiatives like Creative Companions. My passion for debating, public speaking, and philosophy complements my leadership in competitions focused on legal research and simulated hearings. An avid learner with interests in music production, illustration, and sports (having represented India in handball), I thrive at the intersection of technology, creativity, and community engagement.
